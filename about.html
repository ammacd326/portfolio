About Me

Hi, I'm Amy.

I'm a product designer who's really into making complex things feel simple (or at least simpler). I've spent the last 5+ years designing in B2B SaaS, mostly in FinTech and LegalTech, and now in the home services space at JobNimbus.

My path into design was a bit of a pivot. I started out in recruiting, working with tech teams in Salt Lake, before deciding to switch into UX. That experience ended up shaping how I approach design more than anything else. I naturally think a lot about people first: what they need, what's frustrating them, and what actually makes something click.

I like working end-to-end and getting into the details, but I'm also big on stepping back and making sure we're solving the right problem in the first place. Clean, intuitive, and actually useful is always the goal.

Outside of work, I'm usually hanging out with my husband, Dennis, and our dogs, Nova and Barkley, planning trips, golfing, or finding new spots to grab a drink or a bite to eat.
